Alan Leverenz

Born and raised in sunny San Diego, my life in the Big Apple started when I went to graduate school at Teachers College. Living and working in Manhattan has been a great experience. I never seem to tire of the pace and seeing things change. Same goes for my career, which has shape-shifted from teaching through software product management to various roles in advertising technologies.

One thread running through the years has been my fascination with computer technology and the experience of crafting useful solutions being part of a team. It's a great experience constructing something as elegant as it is useful or meaningful.
